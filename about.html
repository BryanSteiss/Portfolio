I'm Bryan Steiss, a second-year IT Web Programming student at NSCC seeking a Junior Web Developer position.

After 20+ years of working in the food industry as a Chef and Cook, I decided I needed something a little more challenging. Knowing absolutely nothing about computers - not even how to use Word or save a file - I enrolled in the Web Programming course at NSCC.

I've come a long way from where I started and have come to love the process of programming. After some hard work and frustration, the feeling of solving a problem is unbeatable. My favourite languages and frameworks to use are PHP, C#, HTML, CSS, WordPress, ASP.net, and Laravel.

I am currently working with 5 of my fellow students on a healthcare app. Well, it's a poop tracking app. The app is targeted towards individuals that may have health concerns that require them to track their bowel movements or really anyone who wants to keep track of their schedule. It's been a blast so far to work on an app that could benefit someone's health one day.

When I'm not programming, you can find me creating visual art, playing softball, or crate-digging for records.
